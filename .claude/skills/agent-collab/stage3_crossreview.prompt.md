# Stage 3: Cross Review

Review the candidate enriched plan against the original draft and preflight.

## Output Contract

Output exactly these sections:

## Strengths

What the candidate plan does well.

## Suggestions

Improvements to clarity, ordering, or safety.

## Additional

Missing items that should be added.

## Recommended Changes

Concrete edits that should be applied in final consolidation.

## Rules

- Be constructive and specific.
- Prioritize correctness, testability, and operational safety.
- Keep recommendations minimal but high impact.
